## Advice: How to Red?

- Motivasi/Dorongan/Niat
	- If you feel like you’ve been receiving a lot of advice of the first type and don’t feel motivated, try hearing “harsher” advice and see how that affects you, and vice versa
- You don’t need any knowledge of advanced algorithms to advance to (purple/orange/red). Learn (binary search/math/creative thinking) and solve more problems, and you’ll improve
	- Generally, this is true. However, don’t interpret this as “avoid learning advanced algorithms”!
- heating up your brain by doing several problems or contests during the days leading up to a contest ... this helps me shift my brain into the right gear to do a contest with peak performance
- Don’t spend too much time searching for the perfect learning method 
- only practiced when he felt like it, but he often thinks of math in passing
	- wiring your brain to think about CP in random idle times helps a lot, especially in the current CP meta where many solutions appear to come out of nowhere
- familiarize yourself with how problems are made, how test cases are generated, and sometimes even forcing yourself to go out of your comfort zone
	- seek to find the most optimal solution to the problem
- You don’t have to master all topics equally. It’s fine to be strong in one topic and weak in another, it’s fine to enjoy a topic and not like another
