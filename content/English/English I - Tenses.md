## Simple Present Tenses

- What it is?
- What the use cases? When we use it?
- How to formula or grammar structure?
	- Possitive/Affirmative? `Subject + ...`
	- Negative? `Subject + ...`
	- Question? `Do(es) + Subject + Base Verb?`
- What is the signal words?
	- adverb of frequency, e.g. always, sometimes, etc.
